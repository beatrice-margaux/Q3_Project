FIRE PREVENTION

HOW TO PREVENT FIRE:

1) Never Leave Cooking Unattended

Leaving cooking unattended can cause fires. Always turn the stove off when leaving or have someone watch over it.

2) Store flammable products correctly

Alcohol, hairspray, and many other common items at home are highly flammable. Make sure they're stored correctly to avoid fires.

3) Maintin your wires

Did you know that broken wires can cause fires? Always make sure that your wires are fixed and are placed where children can't reach.

4) Always have a fire extinguisher around

Let's say you failed to follow the first 3 ways to prevent a fire, and you're having a fire. A fire extinguisher is the last resort. Always have them around, but it's always better to prevent than to stop a fire.
